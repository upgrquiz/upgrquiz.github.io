Instruction

  • 1. To test your knowledge click the "Play" button, you will move on to "Category" page.
  • 2. Choose category of quiz that you want to pass.
  • 3. Pass quiz by considering this facts:
    • 1) 30s to answer for each question;
    • 2) Only one chance to answer to question;
    • 3) Correct answers will not be shown, if you answered incorrectly or didn't answered.
    • 4) Each correct answer gives 10 scores.
  • 4. If you miss-clicked or choose wrong category, you can click the "Back" button, so that you will return to the "Category" page.
  • 5. After taking the quiz, to save a result, enter your username and click the "Save" button (please, consider that you are not allowed to save the result without username). In case you don't satisfy your result, you can click the "Play again" button to pass again the quiz or just go to the home page by "Go home" button.
  • 6. All high scores are saved in "High Score" page, you can go to this page by the b"High Score" button.
